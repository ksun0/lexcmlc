Computer Science - Coding Circle

Are you frustrated that your child is playing too much video games? Do you want to turn your child's passion for playing games into making games? Coding Circle is the right place for your child. In this course, students will create interactive games or storylines that they can share. Students will learn the fundamentals of Computer Science, including core concepts such as expressions, loops, and functions. They will be immersed in a friendly environment where they will cultivate their collaboration and problem-solving skills with the guide of a knowledgeable, experienced, and inclusive instructor. By the end of the course, your child will be able to share their own custom story or game built with software on code.org. Coding Circle is a great gateway to spark the preliminary interest of computer science in young children, where they can then learn more as they grow older.
